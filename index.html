Gameplay

Genshin is an open massive open world game, where you follow the travaller through the world of Teyvat. Immerse yourself self the amazing sound and art style based on different parts of the world. You get the chance to make your dream team with a huge amount of character available. Every 20 days the game gives new content such as events to participate in or new banners to wish for new characters. Also, enjoy the co-op fonction where you can fight boss with friends.
